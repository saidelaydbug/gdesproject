I am going to rate the colors in the rainbow plus a few others.

color rating
red 9/10. very fun to use in drawing and i look good in this color, but is sometimes a bit too angry on the eyes.
orange 6/10. nice, but not outstanding.
yellow 7/10. lovely and warm, but can go wrong pretty easily. (cough)highlighter yellow(cough)
green 5/10. pretty, but can go wrong very easily and is a bit difficult to work with.
blue 9/10. versatile, soothing, and difficult to make ugly.
purple 5/10. good color, but it's easy to get tired of.
pink 10/10. best color ever. you can never go wrong with pink.
brown 5/10. highly depends on what you do with the color; can range from horribly ugly to comfy cozy.
black 7/10. solid color that goes with everything. kind of edgy, though.
white 9/10. solid color that goes with everything, and softer to me than black.
